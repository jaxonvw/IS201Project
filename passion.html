•Winter Activities in Utah•

      Sports

      Ever since I was a child my parents had me in the snow! We would snowshoe, ski, and build igloos. Snow sports are some of my faovrite things ever. I would recommend snow sports to anyone and everyone. They are different, fun, engaing, and overall give you some of the best winter views you will ever see.

      Places to Visit
        Park City

        Park City is home to many amazing ski resorts, snowshoe trails, and eateries in Utah. I personally would reccomend going to the Stein Erickson Buffet.

        Salt Lake

        If you find yourself in SLC in the winter, go check out Temple Square, the lights are amazing!!

        Little Cotton-Wood Canyon

        This one is a no brainer. Just look at this picture, the contrast between the orange sky and white snow is crazy.
